📖 First-Person Mode Usage Instructions

  1. Enable First-Person Mode: Click the "🎯 First-Person Mode" button in the game UI
  2. Look Around: Move your mouse to look around the scene
  3. Interact: Click anywhere to interact with objects at the center of your screen (crosshair)
  4. Collect Stones: Look at stones and click to collect them
  5. Use Catapult: Look at the catapult and click to load/fire stones
  6. Exit Mode: Click the "🏃 Exit First-Person" button to return to normal camera mode
